Why You Should Own a Cat

Cats are one of those animals that are nice to have around with you. Cats are nice to have around because they can do their own things without you. For example, if you are out doing work, cats will just sleep. If you have an auto feeder, they will get their food on their own. They can also use the bathroom on their own as long as they have a kitty litter. You as an owner do not have to do much besides cleaning their area / litter and can play with them occasionally. Another good reason you should own cats is because they are cute. Some cats are also very cuddly and affectionate depending on their personality. Overall, cats are nice to have around and they are cute. Learn about cats at cats.com!
